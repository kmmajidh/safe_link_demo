Simulated suspicious link

Attackers often hide real destinations:

  • Using URL shorteners (bit.ly, tinyurl).
  • Domains that look similar (paypa1.com vs paypal.com).
  • Unexpected downloads/attachments.
Checklist before clicking:
  1. Do you know the sender?
  2. Hover to preview URL (desktop) or long-press (mobile).
  3. Check HTTPS & correct domain.
  4. Don’t download from unknown senders.
  5. Keep antivirus & OS updated.
